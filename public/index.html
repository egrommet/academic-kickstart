Biography

I’m a Senior Lecturer in Data Journalism at Cardiff University’s School of Journalism, Media and Culture.

I’m the co-director of the MSc in Computational and Data Journalism and a co-founder of the European Data and Computational Journalism Conference. My interests include data journalism, journalism and software development, social media and production journalism.
